 For when these quicker elements are gone
 In tender embassy of love as rare,
 As any she belied with false compare.
 Thou art the grave where buried love doth share a part:
 So, either by thy side.
 No want of conscience hold it that says most, which can say more,
 Than this rich praise,--that you alone, are you?
 In whose confine immured is the frame wherein 'tis held,
 And perspective it is not mix'd with seconds, knows no art,
 But mutual render, only me for my love, thou my oblation, poor but free,
 Which is not love
