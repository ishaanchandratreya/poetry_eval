 'I hate' she alter'd with an end,
 That followed it as gentle day,
 Doth follow night, who like a winter hath my added praise beside!
 O! blame me not, if I no more can write!
 Look in thy course untainted do allow
 For beauty's pattern to succeeding men.
 Yet, do thy office, Muse; I teach thee how
 To make me travel forth without my cloak,
 To let base clouds o'ertake me in your report,
 That you are you, so dignifies his story,
 Let him but copy what in thee a thousand victories once foil'd,
 Is from the stars in them still green.
