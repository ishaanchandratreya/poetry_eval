 O! 'tis the first, 'tis flattery in my verse so barren of new pride,
 So far from home into my verse
 Thine own sweet argument, too excellent
 For every vulgar paper to rehearse?
 O! give thy self art so unprovident.
 Grant, if thou live, remember'd not to sell.
 My glass shall not be free,
 For thou not renewest,
 Thou dost beguile the world, or else this glutton be,
