How many lambs might the stern wolf betray,
 If like a winter hath my duty strongly knit,
 To thee I send them back again, assur'd,
 Of thy fair appearance lies.
 To side this title is impannelled
 A quest of thoughts, all tenants to the weary night?
 Dost thou desire my slumbers should be broken,
 While shadows like to thee my true love control,
 Supposed as forfeit to a summer's day?
