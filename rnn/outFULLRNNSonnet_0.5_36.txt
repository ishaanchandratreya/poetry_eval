But heavy tears, badges of either's reign,
Do in consent shake hands to torture me,
The one by toil, the other to sence.
For all the world must fairest in my judgment's place.
In nothing art thou black save in thy deeds,
And thence this slander, and in quest to have extreme;
A bliss in proof, and proved, a very woe;
Before, a joy proposed; behind,
Since that my life on thy revolt doth lie.
