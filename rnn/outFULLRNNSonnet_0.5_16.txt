Thy pill carry me away,
My life hath in this line some interest,
Which for memorial still with thee shall summer's hine
O! never say that I was false of heart,
Though absence seemed my flame to qualify,
As easy might I do so, love; yet when they have devised,
What strained touches rhetoric can lend,
Thou truly fair, whoce me desire keep pace.
Therefore desire, (of perfect'st love being made)
Shall neigh, no dull flesh, in hould others' false adulterate eyes
Give salutation to my sportive blood?
Or on my frailties why are all deyes and women's souls amazeth.
And for a woman wert thou first created;
Till Nature, as she wrought bove and thine image dies with thee.
Unthrifty loveliness, why dost thou spend
Upon thy self thy beauty liked in the even,
Doth half that glory to the sober west,
As those two mourning eyes become thy face:
